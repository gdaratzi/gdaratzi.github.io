Why Choose Us

The Extra virgin organic olive oil Agia is produced in the Municipality of Visaltia in the prefecture of Serres. The fruit is harvested only by hand during the initial stage of ripening and the olive oil is extracted at ambient temperatures, thus preserving all the nutrients and flavor of the fruit.

Under the strict supervision and control of all production stages, our excellent organic olive oil is offered generously on your plate.

We emphasize every stage so that the result would be optimal.

To achieve purity of our product we are verified with all the standards of BIO HELLAS and also we take our feedback for this purity with chemical analysis of many parameters crucial for Organic Olive Oil.
